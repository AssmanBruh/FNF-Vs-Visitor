# Friday Night Funkin' Vs Visitor!

FNF Vs Visitor! is a mod with new songs made for [Friday Night Funkin'](https://github.com/FunkinCrew/Funkin) that adds new weeks. it tells us what would happen if Visitor had landed in the world of FNF, and also includes remixes of some songs, and the cherry on top: the unique feature of being able to play as Visitor by selecting him from the character selector menu, playing the songs from Friday Night Funkin' as Visitor Mixes!
